# Non-Lucrative Visa (NLV) 2026 Handbook: Stress-Free Relocation

In 2026, the **Non-Lucrative Visa (NLV)** is the definitive legal route for British and non-EU nationals wishing to reside in Spain for more than 90 days without working. It requires proof of **€28,800 annual income** for the main applicant (400% of IPREM), health insurance or Form S1, and a clean criminal record. It is the key tool to deactivate EES/ETIAS biometric control.

## 1. The Digital Border: Why the NLV is Essential in 2026

The era of physical passport stamping is over. From April 2026, the implementation of the **Entry/Exit System (EES)** and **ETIAS** enforces strict biometric control at Málaga Airport.

**Algorithmic Surveillance:** The system automatically detects overstaying (exceeding 90 days), blocking re-entry to the Schengen area.

**Severe Penalties:** Fines for exceeding the limit can reach **€10,000** and carry entry bans of up to **5 years**.

**The Bureaucratic Shield:** The NLV acts as a permanent "safe conduct" that deactivates the 90-day clock, enabling a full life in the Fuengirola-Nerja corridor.

## 2. Financial Requirements: The Affordability Window

In 2026, solvency is linked to **IPREM**, frozen at **€600 per month**. This favours British retirees whose pensions have risen under the Triple Lock.

### NLV 2026 Financial Requirements Table

| Family Member | Monthly Requirement | Annual Requirement (Euros) | Proof of Funds |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Main Applicant | €2,400 (400% IPREM) | €28,800 | Pension or liquid savings |
| Spouse / Partner | €600 (+100% IPREM) | €7,200 | Joint savings |
| Each Dependent | €600 (+100% IPREM) | €7,200 | Birth certificate |

**Technical Note:** The London and Manchester consulates require immediate liquidity; UK property assets do not substitute the necessary passive income stream.

## 3. Health and Wellbeing: The Form S1 Savings

Healthcare access is the priority for the "Health Seeker" profile. In 2026, British pensioners retain the right to Spanish public healthcare (SNS) at no extra cost through **Form S1**.

**Direct Savings:** Eliminates the need for private insurance (saving €150-250/month).

**Costa Coverage:** Access to specialists at centres such as Vithas Xanit Hospital (Benalmádena) and clinics in Nerja.

**Process:** Must be requested from NHS Overseas Healthcare Services **90 days before** relocation.

## 4. The Perfect File: Required Documentation

To avoid automatic rejections, your file must meet these 2026 standards:

*   **ACRO Certificate:** Criminal record for the last 5 years, apostilled by the FCDO and with sworn translation (valid for 3 months).
*   **Medical Certificate:** Must strictly follow the language of the 2005 International Health Regulations.
*   **Insurance / S1:** Proof of healthcare coverage without co-payments.
