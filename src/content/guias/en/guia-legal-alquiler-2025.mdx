# 2025 Legal Guide for Property Rental

The rental market is changing and it is essential to stay informed. In this guide, we break down the key points of the new regulations, with a focus on your peace of mind and financial security.

## 1. Changes in Contract Duration

The new law aims to provide greater stability for both owner and tenant.

*   **Long-term contracts:** Contracts of more than 5 years are incentivised with tax benefits.
*   **Automatic extensions:** Now more flexible, allowing recovery of the property for family needs with 2 months' notice.

> "The key to a good rental is a clear contract that protects both parties from day one."

## 2. Payment Guarantees

We know that payment security is your main concern.

1.  **Non-payment insurance:** We strongly recommend taking out non-payment insurance.
2.  **Bank guarantee:** Still a valid option, though less common.
