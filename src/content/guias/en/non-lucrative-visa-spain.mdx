The dynamics of global travel are radically changing, shifting from short holidays toward extended stays driven by climate and lifestyle factors. However, strict immigration rules mean you cannot simply arrive and stay indefinitely.

## 2. Key Financial Requirements

The Spanish government requires proof that you can live comfortably without relying on public funds. To qualify for the NLV, you must demonstrate reliable passive income or sufficient savings:

* **Main Applicant:** Must demonstrate financial requirements of at least €28,800 per year.
* **Dependents:** If bringing a spouse or dependent, you must prove an additional €7,200 per year for each person.

---

## 3. The Application Process: What to Expect

Securing your NLV involves several critical steps that demand precision and the correct legal formatting:

<IconList>
  * **🩺 Comprehensive Private Health Insurance:** You cannot rely on the public health system initially. You must secure a fully comprehensive private policy, valid for the duration of your stay with zero copayments.
  * **📝 Medical Certificate:** A formal letter from a recognized physician proving you do not suffer from any diseases that could have serious repercussions for public health.
  * **⚖️ Criminal Background Check:** Background checks from any country you have lived in over the past five years, properly translated and apostilled.
  * **🏡 Proof of Accommodation:** While not always strictly demanded at step one, having expertly drafted rental contracts that legally protect your stay significantly strengthens your application and avoids legal insecurity.
</IconList>
